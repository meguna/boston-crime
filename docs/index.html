Explore Boston's Crime Data

Yellow dots denote Part 1 crimes.
Green dots denote Part 2 crimes.

Crimes are categorized into "Part 1" and "Part 2" by the FBI's Uniform Crime Reporting Program. Part 1 crimes are generally more violent, and consist of: criminal homicide, forcible rape, robbery, aggrevated assault, burglary, larceny, motor vehicle theft, and arson. Specific definitions of these crimes are provided here.

On this map, crimes are clustered by location and the size of the dot represents the number of crimes in each cluster. (Specifically, the clusters are generated by the mini batch K-means method in SciKit Learn). As you zoom in, more precise data points are shown. At the smallest zoom level, the map shows all three hundred thousand+ data points.

This map is generated by D3.js and HTML5 Canvas for high performance.
